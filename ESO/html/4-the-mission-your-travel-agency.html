4. The mission: your travel agency

Welcome, Marketing Experts!

It’s time to stop being students and start being Creative Directors. Your mission is to put your travel agency on the map. To do this, you will design a professional advertisement th

at captures the essence of adventure and luxury.

🛠️ Our Secret Weapon: CANVA
To achieve a professional result, we will use Canva. We have chosen this platform for your project because:

- Professional Quality: You have access to a massive library of high-quality photos and videos. Remember: In travel, an image is worth a thousand words!
- Safety First: We will use our School Corporate Accounts, ensuring a private and secure digital environment.
- Cloud Collaboration: You can work together in real-time, whether you are in the classroom or at home.
- Creative Scaffolding: Don’t worry if you aren’t a graphic designer yet! Canva offers professional templates to help you build your masterpiece.
 
📝 Your Task: Step-by-Step
To complete "The Mission", your agency must follow these instructions:

1. Identity Check: Choose a name for your agency and a "vibe" (Eco-friendly, Luxury, Adventure, or Budget Travel).
2. Visual Storytelling: Select images that make people want to pack their bags immediately. Use Canva’s filters and tools to create a consistent look.
3. The Power of Words: Write a persuasive message in English. You must include:

- A Catchy Slogan (e.g., "Escape the Ordinary").
- Engaging Descriptions using strong adjectives.
- A Call to Action (e.g., "Book your flight today!").
- Conditional Sentences for special offers (e.g., "If you book before June, you will get a 20% discount").
- Legal & Safe: Ensure all your elements are copyright-free and avoid using real personal data (phones or addresses).

🏆 Goal
Your final product must be a digital poster or a social media ad that looks so real that we all want to buy a ticket from you!

Tip

Check the "Templates" section in Canva and search for "Travel Agency" or "Travel Poster". Use them as a base, but make sure to customize the colors, fonts, and texts to make it unique!
