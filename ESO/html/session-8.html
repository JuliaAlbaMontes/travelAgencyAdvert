Session 8

Oral Presentation of the Advert

- Task: The time has come to present your advertisement to your classmates. In the attached link, you will find a Mentimeter so you can evaluate the other groups.
- Grouping: Groups of 3 students.
- Setting: Regular classroom with a digital whiteboard.
- Technical Resources: Digital whiteboard.
- Tools: Your final Canva design projected for everyone to see.

Check the following link to access Mentimeter and assess your classmates: https://www.menti.com/albg4qpeivyi 

Once you have finished your Travel Agency project, it's time to look back and see how much you have grown.

Instead of a boring survey, we will use a Learning Target. This visual tool helps you (and me!) understand your experience during the project.

🗺️ How does it work?
You will receive a digital target divided into 4 key areas. You must color each section from the center (1 - I struggled) to the outer edge (4 - I feel like a pro!).

🔍 The 4 Areas of your Target:
Canva Mastery: How comfortable did you feel designing your ad?
English Fluency: Did you feel confident using persuasive language and travel vocabulary?
Digital Rights (Copyright): Are you sure about how to find and use legal images?
Teamwork & Deadlines: How well did your agency collaborate and manage time?
💡 Why are we doing this?
This isn't for a grade! This is for YOU to see your progress and for ME to know where you need more support. If the whole class feels "in the center" regarding English, I will know we need more practice with those grammar structures next time!

Action: Use the Canva template provided, color your target, and upload it to our virtual classroom.
